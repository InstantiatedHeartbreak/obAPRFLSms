In Loving Memory of Marc Stremciuc

2004 - 2025

It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Marc Stremciuc on March 30, 2025. He loved to play bass, watch hockey, and edit Wikipedia.

After a long battle with pancreatic cancer, he died in his home surrounded by friends on January 15, 2025.

Marc Stremciuc is survived by his brothers, Joshua and Joshua 2. He will be remembered for successfully constructing a Luty at 20.

After a long battle with being hit by an eighteen-wheeler, he died on the side of the road surrounded by friends on March 30th, 2025.

A memorial service will be held on 4/20 at Araach Linde Funeral Home. All are welcome to attend and celebrate his life.

After a long battle with the 2nd Battalion 1st Marines, he lost his position and died in a bunker surrounded by the 2nd Battalion 1st Marines on June 14th, 1981.

In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to their pockets.
