In Loving Memory of Marc Stremciuc

2004 - 2025

It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Marc Stremciuc on March 30, 2025. He loved to play bass, watch hockey, and edit Wikipedia.

Marc Stremciuc is survived by his brothers, Joshua and Josh. He will be remembered for successfully constructing a Luty at 20.

A memorial service will be held on 4/20 at Araach Linde Funeral Home. All are welcome to attend and celebrate his life.

In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to their pockets.
