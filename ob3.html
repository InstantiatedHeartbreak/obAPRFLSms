In Loving Memory of Morgan Spurlock

1970 - 2024

Morgan Spurlock died last year and he was actually awesome so this part of the page is not a joke.

He made Super Size Me, that documentary where he ate Mcdonald's every day for a month. It actually changed the world.

He was an alcoholic, though, and the results of his experiment were skewed by that and his drug use.

He also got wrapped up in the #MeToo movement, so maybe he wasn't that awesome- BUT his documentary was great to watch.
